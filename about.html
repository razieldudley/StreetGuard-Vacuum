About Street Guard Vacuum

Our mission to create safer, cleaner communities for families everywhere

Our Story

Street Guard Vacuum was born from a simple observation: neighborhoods were struggling with pollution that traditional cleaning methods couldn't handle effectively.

I saw families concerned about their children playing in areas littered with cigarette butts, vapes, bottles, and other harmful debris. Existing solutions weren't designed to tackle these specific pollutants that pose real health risks to our communities.

That's when I developed our revolutionary vacuum technology, a solution specifically engineered to capture and remove the pollution that matters most to community safety.

Business Plan Overview

Street Guard Vacuum is built to solve polluted streets, hazardous debris, and public safety concerns with a subscription-based cleaning system.

Identity

StreetGuard Vacuum is my business that develops advanced cleaning technology designed to remove pollution and eliminate harmful objects from community streets to create safer, healthier environments for our residents.

Problem

Communities struggle with polluted streets and hazardous debris and trash that threaten public health, environment, and safety.

Solution

An advanced vacuum system that removes pollution and safely eliminates hazardous debris like cigs and vapes from streets and public spaces.

Target Market

Adults in their 25-40s such as sanitation managers and community leaders who prioritize public environments.

Revenue Streams

StreetGuard earns money from subscriptions where customers pay a recurring fee weekly or monthly to have ongoing access to our service. Pricing starts at $35 for a weekly subscription and $140 for the best monthly deal.
