About Street Guard Vacuum

Our mission to create safer, cleaner communities for families everywhere

Our Story

Street Guard Vacuum was born from a simple observation: neighborhoods were struggling with pollution that traditional cleaning methods couldn't handle effectively.

I saw families concerned about their children playing in areas littered with cigarette butts, vapes, bottles, and other harmful debris. Existing solutions weren't designed to tackle these specific pollutants that pose real health risks to our communities.

That's when I developed our revolutionary vacuum technology, a solution specifically engineered to capture and remove the pollution that matters most to community safety.
