What Street Guard Vacuum Is

An advanced vacuum system that removes pollution and safely eliminates hazardous debris like cigarette butts and vape cartridges from streets, sidewalks, plazas, and public spaces.

Designed for business districts, property managers, HOAs, and community organizers, our service eliminates urban pollution without the dust and danger of traditional broom-and-sweep cleanup.

  • Captures hazardous debris before it reaches storm drains
  • Protects storefronts, sidewalks, and pedestrian areas
  • Makes public spaces safer for families, workers, and visitors
